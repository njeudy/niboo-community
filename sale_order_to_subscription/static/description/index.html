User guide

How to create a subscription from a sale order?

  1. Go to Sales > Sales > Sales Orders
  2. Select a subscription product in the sale order lines
  3. Make sure you select an Analytic Account (e.g. a Project)
  4. Save and click Confirm Sale

How to create a subscription product?

  1. Go to Sales > Sales > Products
  2. Go to the Sales tab on the desired product
  3. Check Subscription Product
